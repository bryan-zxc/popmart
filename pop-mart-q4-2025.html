Omnichannel Retail Network

Pop Mart operates 512 retail stores globally, complemented by 2,100+ roboshops (smart vending machines), and a rapidly growing digital ecosystem across e-commerce platforms and mobile apps.

The integration of physical and digital touchpoints creates a seamless shopping experience, with 73% of customers engaging across multiple channels during their purchase journey.

Store Formats: Flagship stores (20+ locations), Standard stores (350+), Pop-up stores (140+), and Roboshops (2,100+).

Achievements

Milestones and strategic accomplishments

Q4 2025 Key Achievements

  • Record Financial Performance: Achieved ¥8.2B in quarterly revenue, representing 48% year-over-year growth and marking the strongest quarter in company history
  • Global Flagship Expansion: Successfully opened landmark stores in London Covent Garden and Dubai Mall, establishing premium brand presence in key Western markets
  • Studio Ghibli Partnership: Launched groundbreaking collaboration with Studio Ghibli, generating record pre-orders and expanding brand appeal to mainstream audiences
  • Digital Transformation: Enhanced omnichannel capabilities with 58% online revenue growth and improved mobile app experience driving 4.2M new member acquisitions
  • Operational Excellence: Improved gross margin to 64% through supply chain optimization and premium product mix while maintaining brand quality standards
  • International Momentum: International revenue reached 28% of total sales with 73% growth, validating global expansion strategy and market-fit across diverse geographies
